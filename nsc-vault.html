Your Study Plan

How to Use the Vault

01

Pick Your Subject

Start with the subject you find hardest or the one your exam is closest. Each subject has its own dedicated vault with all materials organised by topic.

02

Study the Summary Notes

Go through the CAPS-aligned topic summaries first to build or refresh your understanding. Use the mind maps to see how concepts connect.

03

Test Yourself

Do the practice quizzes for each topic before moving on. Then work through full past papers under timed conditions to simulate the real exam.

04

Review with the Memos

Mark your own work using the official memoranda. Understand where marks are lost — the memo is your most powerful study tool.
